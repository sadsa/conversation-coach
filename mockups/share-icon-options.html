Quick steer. iOS share (A) is the safest pick — universally legible, family-consistent, doesn't lie about which apps the share-target accepts. WhatsApp brand-green (B) wins on raw recognition and matches the deep-link target (the onboarding step is literally the WhatsApp share illustration), but it's the only branded mark in the app and visually loudest. WhatsApp in accent (C) is the compromise that satisfies neither argument completely. If you want a single recommendation: A.
