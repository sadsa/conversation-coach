Things to watch for as you choose. The Review icon sits between a microphone and a book — those are both fairly tall, organic silhouettes. The candidates that read most clearly tend to be squat and rectangular (article, file-text, tray, files) because they don't fight the surrounding shapes. Notebook and note-pencil get close enough to Study's book that the eye can confuse them at speed. Chat-family glyphs are great if Review reads as "the conversations you've recorded," but be aware Practise already owns a speaking metaphor (microphone), so two bubbles in the same nav can muddle the model.
