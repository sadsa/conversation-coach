Settings

How to pick. The active state is what the user reads first — a tab that doesn't change shape between inactive and active will always feel weaker than one that fills. If you stay on Lucide-style strokes (today), consider painting the active glyph with a tint or background pill instead of relying on colour alone. If you switch to Phosphor or Material Symbols, the fill-on-active behaviour is free.
